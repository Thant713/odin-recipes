over medium egg

Quick and easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter or 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 egg
  • Kosher salt and black pepper

Cook

  1. preheat pan w/ butter/oil for ? mins on medium heat(5)
  2. add egg
  3. season w/ salt+pepper for 1 min
  4. flip and cook for 1 min
  5. plate
